Consumer Credit Scoring APIs

APIs for personal loan, credit card, and consumer credit assessment

  • Bank Statement Analysis Analyze bank transactions to extract 38+ financial features
  • ITR Analysis Validate income from Income Tax Returns
  • Credit Report Analysis Analyze credit bureau reports (CIBIL/Experian)
  • Asset Analysis Analyze customer assets and investments
  • Final Credit Score Combine all analyses to generate final credit score

MSME Credit Scoring APIs

APIs for business loans, working capital, and MSME credit assessment

  • GST Analysis Analyze GST returns for revenue and compliance assessment
  • Director Personal Banking Analyze director's personal bank statements
  • Business Banking Analyze business current account for cash flow
  • Final MSME Credit Score Combine all analyses for business credit score with DSCR
